Message Of Department Head
Engr. Rownak Ara Chowdhury
Assistant Professor & Head of the Department of CSE

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Mymensingh Engineering College(MEC).Our department provides an outstanding opportunity to students to get quality education in Computer Science and Engineering.
Computer Science is an exciting, challenging and dynamic discipline. Computers form an integral part in every aspect of modern life introducing new computing technologies at an enormous rate. To develop new technologies, Computer Science is a dynamic and challenging field of study which changes continually and rapidly. Being one of the leading Engineering College in Bangladesh, Dept. of CSE, MEC has always led the change with constant revision of goals and realistic plans to achieve them.
Our department has been widely recognized for its excellent research and teaching capabilities. The graduates from the department are heavily recruited by both academia and industry of home and abroad. We are very proud of our graduates and their accomplishments; they are highly skilled IT professionals who pursue successful careers in a wide range of contexts in the competitive global labor market.

About Us:-

Department of Computer Science and Engineering of MEC, started its academic activities from January 11, 2018 with the aim of quality education as well as cutting edge researches in the branches of Computer Science and Engineering. Currently this department provide Undergraduate (BSc. Engineering) program. Every year only top ranked 60 brilliant students are enrolled in this department through a competitive admission test. The department has well equipped different Laboratories and a System such as Networking Lab, Data Communication Lab, Microprocessor & Microcontroller Lab, Digital Logic Design Lab, Central Computer Lab, Software Lab, Computer Club Lab & Digital Image Processing Lab to increase the expertise of students practically along with theoretical knowledge.
Dept. of CSE has always been widely recognized for its excellent research and teaching capabilities. The main strength of the department is the talented faculty members who are dedicated in teaching and research.
The department of CSE regularly arranges co-curricular activities such as workshops/Seminars, Lecture series, Practical demos & CSE Carnival. The student body of the department namely Computer Club of (CSE). This Computer Club is active in organizing co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Its organizes programming contests regularly through “Special Group of Interest in Programming Contest” (SGIPC) to prepare the students for the ACM/ICPC collegiate and other competitive programming contests. It also organize hands-on workshops on robotics, embedded systems development and many more. They are also devoted to organize the sports and cultural activities of this department.

Vision of The Department:-

"To become a Center of Excellence in the field of Computer Science and Engineering with a strong research and teaching environment that adapts swiftly to the challenges of the Computer Technology Evolution"

Mission of The Department:-

1. To provide qualitative education and generate new knowledge by engaging in cutting-edge research, and by offering state-of-the-art undergraduate, leading to careers as "Computer Science and Engineering" professionals in the widely diversified domains of industry, government and academia.
2. To promote a teaching and learning process that yields advancements in state-of-the-art in computer science and engineering, and technology, resulting in integration of research results and innovations into other engineering faculty leading to new technologies and products.
3. To harness human capital for sustainable competitive edge and social relevance by inculcating the philosophy of life-long learning and innovation in Computer Science and Engineering.
